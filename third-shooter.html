Third Person Shooter

Shooter game with basic AI and shooting mechanics

About the Game

Shooting

The Shooting system in the game is done using projection. A vector is projected from the muzzle of the gun in the direction of the normal of the the muzzle. Whenever a collision is detected in the line that is traced, we get the imapct vector. This is where we can spawn impact animation.

Artificial Intelligence

The AI is divided into two parts.
The Path Tracing where the AI follows a valid path to the required destination. This is done by giving the AI a valid space to walk and a target. When the player comes into the sight of the AI, it starts walking towards the player.
The Behaviour trees which control how an AI behaves when an event occurs. For example, when it sees the player, an event occurs and the AI is made to walk towards the player. When it reaches a set distance the AI starts shooting. It also follows a patrol path that is goes in when the payer is not in sight.

Health System

Health system is build using impact collision on the player and the AI. Both the player and the AI are given a health bar and whenever they come in contact with a bullet, their health is deducted.
