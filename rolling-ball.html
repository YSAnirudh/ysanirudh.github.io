Rolling Ball

Game with multiple levels

About the Game

Movement

The movement of the ball is made by applying torque in the direction needed accoring to the WASD keys. It acts as a normal sphere when it interacts with objects. For example, when an upward force is applied to the ball, the ball is tossed high into the air.

Buttons and Bombs

The buttons or triggers are made to perform different activities from activating a tile, tossing the ball up, lifting up a door, etc. The bomb, when the player interacts, pushes the player backwards and reduces their health. And the health pikup restores a bit of the player's health.

Levels

The levels in the game are handled by saving the state of the game. That is the important variables that determine how the levels operate and how the player behaves. When a new level is loaded, the game state is also loaded from the previously saved state. This means the player goes to the next level without losing progress.

Health and Progress bar

The player has a health bar to determine whether he loses the game. Player's health decreases when they interact with bombs and increases when they interact with health pickups. The progress bar shows if the player has collected all the coins in the level or not.
